Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions below.

What is TIU Impact Hub?

TIU Impact Hub is a student-led organization at Tokyo International University focused on fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership among business students.

How can I join TIU Impact Hub?

You can join TIU Impact Hub by signing up on our website. Click the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page to get started.

What events does TIU Impact Hub organize?

We organize a variety of events, including workshops, hackathons, pitch competitions, and networking sessions. These events are designed to help students enhance their entrepreneurial skills and connect with industry leaders.

How can I participate in events?

You can participate in our events by registering through our events page. Visit the "Events" section on our website to learn more and sign up for upcoming events.
